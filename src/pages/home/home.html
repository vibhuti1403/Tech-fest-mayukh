About

Mayukh is back, multifold the fanfare and fun. This technical fiesta of Banasthali Vidhyapith is an awaited and alluring journey of three days for female technocrats from the University and the autonomous institutes across the country, and has been successful in paving its path to carry the uniqueness of Asia's largest residential women University's Tech Fest. This year Mayukh will be held from 10th March to 12th March and the theme for this year's extravaganza is Invictus: Undefeatable. Mayukh serves as a perfect launchpad for technology enthusiasts to showcase their intelligence and inventiveness and a massive platform for various activities, organized in the form of competitive events, guest lectures, technical workshops and exhibition. Be it technology or be it art, be it mathematics or be it photography, this event has something or the other to satisfy the varied palates of all the participants. The cultural night encapsulates a sequence of stunning performances that will win you all over and make your body sway to the beats. Mayukh has kept it's legacy by recieving admirable response and the event is growing in stature and popularity ever since. Be dexterous and put your best foot forward, because one thing we assure you of, that these three days will fill you with sheer joy and happiness, making it one of the memorable events in your life. We cordially invite you to come forth and be a part of this three day technical fiesta,Mayukh'19. We look forward to your participation.

General Instructions

  • Only female participants are eligible for the Tech Fest.
  • A Team can comprise of minimum 3 members and maximum of 5 members.
  • Registration fees Rs 100/- per team for Internal Teams.
  • Registration fees Rs 500/- per team for External Teams (including food and acoomodation).
  • Registration fees does not include workshop(s) fee.
  • Each team must make the payment in order to register for events.
  • It is mandatory for all the participants to bring Institute Identity card of their institute as well as Transaction Slip.
  • External Team can come with one or two faculty from their University.

Frequently Asked Questions


Q: After registering,what if we want to extend team size further on?

A: You cannot change the team size after registration.

Q: Can we make further updations in the events of already registered team?

A: No, you can change your names during the events. But only the member of the same team can replace the other.

Q: We are group of friends not participating in any of the major competitions and just coming to Mayukh to have fun. Would we be allowed?

A: No.

Q: Can two participants be from different colleges in a team event?

A: No, it is not possible.
